Metal Deposits In The United States

Mining has been a key component of the economy of the United States with a gross contribution of over 400 billion to U.S GDP in 2020 & more than 500,000 active employees. The United States is the third largest country in the world with a total area of nearl 10 Million square kilometres & there are diverse metal deposits located all across the Country. The United States Geological Survey recored around 170,000 individual deposits of various Metals such as Gold , Iron & Uranium.
